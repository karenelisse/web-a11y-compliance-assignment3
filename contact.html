Dog Walking Information

Dog Walking Hours

Tues - Sun from 11:00am to 2:30pm

DoggieRescue is open for dog walking!

DoggieRescue is delighted to be able to offer walking with our rescue dogs to the general public. This allows those without experience to gain some with dogs or those who cannot have a dog to get their doggie fix!

When you come to walk at Doggie Rescue, please note some important things

  • You must sign in via the Service NSW QR Code
  • You must sign in via the DoggieRescue laptop
  • You must wear enclosed shoes (for safety!)
  • If this is your first time walking you must bring photo ID and there is a one off $20 fee
  • Please be aware we have limited child friendly dogs at the moment so there may be a delay for those walking with children.

If you have any questions please don't hesitate to call us on 02 9486 3133
